Frequently Asked Questions

Why not offer a free tier?
Free apps need revenue. They get it by selling your data to advertisers or using aggressive tracking. Calendar events are deeply personal - they reveal your location, schedule, contacts, and habits. For less than $1/month, we can build features you want without compromising your privacy.
Is there really a 7-day free trial?
Yes! No credit card required. Download the extension, try all features for 7 days completely free. We'll email you 2 days before the trial ends. Cancel anytime with one click.
What if I'm not satisfied?
30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Just email support@calclik.app and we'll process your refund immediately through Stripe.
Can I use CalClik on multiple devices?
Yes! One subscription covers unlimited devices. Your license syncs automatically across all your browsers.
How does the AI work without sending my data to servers?
CalClik downloads a compact AI model (~50MB) to your browser on first use. All event detection happens locally on your device. Your calendar data never leaves your computer.
Do you really not collect any data?
We only store: your email address (for license delivery), subscription status, and payment info (handled by Stripe - we never see your credit card). No tracking cookies, no analytics, no event data. Our code is open source - verify our claims yourself.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through Stripe. Supports international currencies and handles tax automatically.
Will the price ever increase?
Your price is locked in as long as you maintain your subscription. New subscribers may see different pricing in the future, but existing customers keep their original rate.
