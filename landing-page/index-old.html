Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Processing Only

CALCLiK is designed with privacy-first principles. All AI processing happens locally in your browser using Transformers.js. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

1.2 Extension Data

The browser extension only accesses webpage content you explicitly scan. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

1.3 Analytics (Optional)

We may collect anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics to improve the service. This includes:

  • Extension installation and usage counts
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Error reports (no personal data)

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use information solely to:

  • Provide event detection and calendar integration
  • Improve AI accuracy and performance
  • Ensure compatibility across browsers

2.2 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties, except for:

  • Calendar platforms you choose to integrate with
  • Legal requirements (if applicable)

3. Data Security

3.1 Local Processing

All AI processing occurs locally in your browser, ensuring your data never leaves your device during event extraction.

3.2 Calendar Integration

When you choose to add events to external calendars, you are redirected to those services' official interfaces. We do not store or intercept this data.

3.3 Browser Storage

The extension may store preferences locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device.

4. Your Rights

4.1 Data Control

Since we don't collect personal data, you maintain full control over your information:

  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Clear browser data to reset preferences
  • Disable analytics in extension settings

4.2 GDPR Compliance

Our privacy-first design ensures GDPR compliance by default, as we process minimal data locally.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of CALCLiK constitutes acceptance of any changes.
