To capture the hero screenshot:

1. Open Chrome/Brave browser
2. Load the extension from /Applications/calclik-chrome-extension
3. Navigate to the test page: file:///Applications/calclik-chrome-extension/test-page.html
4. Click the CalClik extension icon to open the popup
5. Take a screenshot showing:
   - The test page with events in the background
   - The CalClik popup with the Tech Conference 2025 event displayed
   - All event details clearly visible
6. Save as: hero-screenshot.png (1200x800px recommended)
7. Move to: /Users/pounds_1/Development/CALCLiK/landing-page/images/

Or use the provided screenshot from the user's attachment!
